# Business Projects

This folder contains real-world data analytics projects based on business datasets.

The projects focus on applying statistical analysis, data visualization and machine learning techniques to solve practical business problems.

## Note

Some projects in this portfolio are based on real business scenarios.

To protect business confidentiality, the datasets included in this repository have been anonymized and/or modified. The analyses, methods and conclusions reflect real analytical workflows, while the published data do not represent actual business figures.
